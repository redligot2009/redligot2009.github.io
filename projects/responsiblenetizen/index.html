How to be a Responsible Netizen

Scroll down to begin

Respect For Others

Refrain from thinking you can change a person’s views, politics or religious beliefs and instead, respect them, as they should respect you. Though you may want to influence people with your beliefs, forcing them to share your opinion, or bashing them won’t do good for you or for your cause. In fact, your ability to influence others itself would be sabotaged by displaying such a lack of respect in commenting. All you will do is give yourself a bad reputation.

"Do unto others as you would have them do unto you"
― Matthew 7:12

Respect Yourself

Your name is the same as your face. Respect it. It doesn’t matter whether it’s a screen name or your real one, it’s still your reputation you are ruining when you comment or post without respect. Be careful with the information, the words and the pictures you post online. There are things better kept to yourself.

“Respect yourself and others will respect you.”
― Confucius, Sayings of Confucius

Keep an Open Mind

Online media exposes us to a wide variety of opinions that we may not agree with. As responsible netizens, we must be able to accept the simple fact that we can't all agree on everything. Regardless of whether or not we agree with each other, a responsible netizen should listen to all sides of an issue in order to make informed decisions that aren't affected by bias.

“A mind is like a parachute. It doesn't work if it is not open.”
― Frank Zappa

Remember these three, and browse responsibly!
